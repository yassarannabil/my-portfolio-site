ZOONOMIND - Early Detection Platform for Zoonotic Diseases

ZOONOMIND is an early detection platform for zoonotic diseases, designed to analyze and predict disease outbreaks using machine learning. By integrating real-time data from various sources, including environmental, animal, and human health data, ZOONOMIND tracks disease patterns and identifies high-risk areas. Currently focused on Dengue Fever, the system provides spatially mapped insights through GIS, enabling timely responses from health authorities.

Analysis of Societal Occupation Shift in Response to AI Development

This research paper explores public sentiment on Twitter regarding the potential for AI to eliminate jobs. Using sentiment analysis, the study analyzes over 185 tweets to gauge the emotional tone surrounding AI’s impact on the future of work. The analysis revealed a predominant negative sentiment, indicating concerns about job displacement and the future of human roles in an increasingly automated world. Through data visualization, including bar plots and word clouds, the study highlights common fears and uncertainties tied to AI's role in society.
